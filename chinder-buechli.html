During my apprenticeship, I had the opportunity to realize two projects in collaboration with 'Eltern-Lehre': the 'Wander-Büechli', featuring children's rhymes and hiking routes, and the 'Füür-Büechli', which teaches children how to handle fire safely through play. Concept, design and image editing were entirely my responsibility — both books were sold at local bookstores.
